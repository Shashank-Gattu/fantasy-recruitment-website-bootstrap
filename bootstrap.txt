px and py are used for padding sides and padding top and bottom respectively.
mt is margin top.
mb is margin bottom.
ms is margin start/left.
me is margin end/right. (same for padding)

similar to using <a> tag . (used in project-6)
set class="modal fade" for smoother transition.

display-1 and display-5 are text display styles.
